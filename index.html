About Us

Sanjeevini Charity has been started in 2014 with 4 voluntary donations. The main focus, right from it's inception, has been providing care and support to financially very poor aged persons and disabled persons, who needs genuine help. We have committed to expand it with help of our friends & families around the globe.

Aim

Our aim is to help needy aged persons and disabled persons irrespective of caste or creed by providing food, shelter, clothing, medical care and other activities like yoga, physical exercises and education to promote their self confidence.

Our long term goal

Construct a happy Old Age Home.

Support Us

We, in all humility, urge you to donate generously to this noble cause as service to the aged & disabled people is a sacred mission of anybody life. Please contact us if you wish to donate any amount, doesn't matter how small it is, every little helps. If you have any enquiries, please contact us.
